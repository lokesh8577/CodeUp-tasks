About CodeFiesta 4.0

Launched in 2022, CodeFiesta has rapidly evolved into a celebration of innovation, passion, and creativity—bringing together the brightest young minds to push the boundaries of technology. With three electrifying editions behind us—CodeFiesta 1.0 with 100+ teams, CodeFiesta 2.0 with 120+ teams, and the record-breaking CodeFiesta 3.0 with 200+ teams—the legacy now continues with an even more ambitious chapter: CodeFiesta 4.0.

This year promises to be bigger, bolder, and more thrilling than ever before. Over a nonstop 24-hour hackathon, brilliant ideas will transform into real-world solutions as imagination collides with innovation. More than just a competition, CodeFiesta is an unforgettable journey of coding, creativity, and collaboration.

Whether you are a seasoned coder or a passionate beginner, CodeFiesta 4.0 is your stage to test your limits, showcase your talent, and build solutions that truly matter. The energy will be electrifying, the challenges extraordinary, and the rewards unforgettable. CodeFiesta 4.0 is not just an event—it is where innovation is born, collaborations thrive, and legends are made.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the hackathon

Who can participate in CodeFiesta?

CodeFiesta is open to all students currently enrolled in a college or university. Both undergraduate and graduate students are welcome to participate. Teams can have 2-4 members.

Do I need to have prior experience?

While some coding experience is helpful, we welcome participants of all skill levels. We'll have mentors available throughout the event to help you with your project, and there will be workshops for beginners.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

You should bring your laptop, charger, any hardware you plan to use, and a valid student ID. We recommend bringing a change of clothes and toiletries if you plan to stay overnight.

How much does it cost to participate?

CodeFiesta is completely free for all participants! We'll provide meals, snacks, and beverages throughout the event, along with swag and prizes.

What if I don't have a team?

No problem! We'll have team formation activities at the beginning of the event to help individuals find teams. You can also participate solo if you prefer.
